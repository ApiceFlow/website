SG Santé Health App

The SG Santé app was designed and developed entirely by our in-house team of seasoned experts. Leveraging over a decade of experience in mobile app development, healthtech integration, and secure data systems, our developers, designers, and product strategists worked closely with the client to bring this innovative healthcare solution to life.

From backend integration with complex insurance systems to intuitive UX for policyholders of all ages, every aspect of the app was crafted with precision, scalability, and compliance in mind. Our team implemented secure offline access, biometric authentication, and GDPR-compliant data handling—ensuring both convenience and trust for end users.

This project is a strong testament to our capability in delivering enterprise-grade mobile solutions that are both technically robust and user-friendly.

Challenges:
  • Complex Healthcare Integration

    • Integrating with multiple third-party health insurance systems and data providers (for reimbursements, third-party payment cards, etc.) required secure, real-time access to sensitive health and policy data.

  • Offline Accessibility Requirements

    • The client needed key features like the third-party payment card and QR code to function without internet connectivity, which raised performance and caching challenges.

  • Data Privacy & Compliance

    • Handling sensitive personal and medical data meant the app had to fully comply with GDPR regulations and adhere to strict data encryption and access controls.

  • Scalable Architecture for B2B Clients

    • The app needed to support multiple insurance plans and personalized features for each policyholder while maintaining high performance across a growing user base.

  • User Adoption Across Demographics

    • The app had to be intuitive for users of all ages, especially older demographics who may not be tech-savvy but need frequent access to their healthcare information.

    Solutions:
  • Robust Backend Integration

    • Developed secure RESTful APIs and real-time sync with SG Santé’s backend systems using OAuth 2.0 authentication and role-based access controls. Implemented fail-safes for API fallbacks and retries.

  • Smart Caching & Offline Support

    • Leveraged secure local storage and intelligent cache expiration to ensure key features (e.g., virtual payment card, QR codes) were always available offline. Integrated biometric authentication for quick, secure access.

  • Compliance-Driven Architecture

    • Applied end-to-end data encryption (AES-256), secure document uploads, and fine-grained permission layers. All data-handling flows were designed to be GDPR-compliant.

  • Modular, Scalable Design

    • Built a flexible app architecture using React Native (or Kotlin/Swift, if native) with dynamic configuration modules to support various policyholder types, plan rules, and UI variations per user.

  • User-Centric UX/UI Design

    • Conducted user research and built an accessible, responsive interface with large touch targets, intuitive navigation, and multi-language support (e.g., French/English toggle).

    Results:
    • 100,000+ downloads on Google Play with strong month-over-month growth
    • Reduced support calls by 40% by empowering users to manage their cards and documents independently
    • 50% faster hospital authorization process through in-app requests
    • 100% GDPR compliance with encrypted data, biometric login, and user-controlled data deletion
    • High engagement with daily usage from thousands of policyholders and active document submissions
    • Trusted by AGEO to manage critical B2B and B2C insurance workflows digitally
