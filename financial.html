Security-by-design is baked into our SDLC: strong encryption, secure key management, role-based access, automated compliance checks and controls mapped to standards such as PCI-DSS, GDPR, SOC2 and regional banking rules; we also perform threat modeling, code reviews, pen tests and audit-ready documentation.
