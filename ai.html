We handle end-to-end projects including LLM integrations and RAG, custom supervised/unsupervised models, computer vision (detection/segmentation), time-series & forecasting, recommendation systems, and embedded/edge ML.

We start with a Discovery & Strategy phase (problem framing, data audit, success metrics), then move to iterative model development + validation, and finish with productionization and MLOps—engagements can be structured as fixed-scope discovery, sprint-based development, or managed services.

We commonly work with finance/FinTech, healthcare & life sciences, retail/e-commerce, manufacturing & industrial IoT, logistics, and SaaS/tech companies that need production-grade AI.

We apply secure pipelines (encryption in transit/at rest), data minimization, role-based access, audit logging, and compliance controls (HIPAA/PCI/GDPR readiness) and can sign NDAs and data-processing addendums as required.

We productionize models using reproducible CI/CD pipelines, containerized or serverless serving, model registries, monitoring for performance & drift, automated retraining triggers, and SLAs for support and incident response.

We offer flexible pricing: fixed-price discovery, time-and-materials sprints, or outcome-linked engagements; ROI is tracked with agreed KPIs (accuracy, automation rate, cost savings, revenue lift, time-to-decision) defined during discovery.
