StormX

StormX is a blockchain-powered rewards platform that allows users to earn cryptocurrency cashback while shopping online. By partnering with over 1,000 retailers such as Nike, eBay, and Microsoft, StormX enables users to earn crypto rewards ranging from 0.5% to over 87.5%, depending on their membership level. The platform is accessible via a mobile app and a Chrome extension, making it convenient for users to earn rewards during their everyday shopping activities.

Technology Stacks:

To ensure a seamless and engaging user experience, we employed the following technologies:

  • Frontend: React.js for a dynamic and responsive user interface.
  • Backend: Node.js with Express for efficient server-side operations.
  • Blockchain Integration: Ethereum for secure and transparent transactions.
  • Database: MongoDB for flexible and scalable data storage.
  • Cloud Services: AWS for reliable cloud infrastructure.
  • Authentication: OAuth for secure user authentication.
    • Features:
  • Crypto Cashback: Earn cryptocurrency rewards on purchases made at participating retailers.
  • Gamified Microtasks:Complete simple tasks such as watching videos or taking surveys to earn additional rewards.
  • Staking: Stake STMX tokens to unlock higher cashback percentages and additional benefits.
  • Referral Program: Refer friends and earn 10% of their shopping and staking base rewards.
  • Multi-Platform Access: Available as a mobile app and a Chrome extension for easy access across devices.
  • Gamification & Rewards: Incentivizes employees with instant HealthCoin to boost engagement.
  • Secure Transactions: Utilize Ethereum's blockchain for secure and transparent reward distribution.
    • Use Case:

    StormX serves as a comprehensive platform for individuals seeking to earn cryptocurrency rewards through everyday activities. It's particularly beneficial for:

  • Online Shoppers: Earning crypto cashback on purchases from a wide range of retailers.
  • Crypto Enthusiasts: Engaging with blockchain technology through a user-friendly interface.
  • Frequent Shoppers: Maximizing rewards through staking and referrals.
    • Challenges:
  • Integration with Multiple Retailers: Seamlessly connecting with over 1,000 online retailers while tracking purchases and applying rewards accurately.
  • User Engagement:Designing an intuitive and engaging interface that encourages users to participate in microtasks and utilize the platform regularly.
  • Secure and Scalable Transactions: Ensuring secure transactions and the ability to handle a large volume of microtransactions efficiently.
  • Cross-Platform Accessibility: Providing a consistent user experience across both mobile and desktop platforms.
    • Solutions:
  • Robust Integration System: Developed a robust tracking and integration system that works across various affiliate networks, ensuring reliable crypto cashback activation.
  • Gamified Microtasks: Implemented gamified elements such as experience points, task levels, and skill development to enhance user engagement.
  • Blockchain Integration: Utilized Ethereum's blockchain for secure and transparent reward distribution, implementing a hybrid solution of off-chain and on-chain transactions to improve scalability.
  • Responsive Design: Created a unified design system and optimized app functionalities for both mobile and desktop platforms to maintain consistency and ease of use.
    • Results:
    • 73% increase in user sign-ups.
    • 75% boost in cashback activity.
    • 86% improvement in user retention.
    • Successfully handled over a million transactions per month.
    • Expanded user base to over 250,000 active users across 180 countries.
