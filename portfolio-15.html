Digital Sumo

Digital Sumo is a marketing analytics and tracking platform focusing on multi-channel attribution—helping businesses analyze campaign performance across ad networks, conversion funnels, server-side tracking, and user journey data. It empowers clients to understand ROI, optimize ad spend, and drive higher conversion rates.

Technology Stacks:

To ensure a seamless and engaging user experience, we employed the following technologies:

  • Frontend: React.js (SPA interactions), Next.js (for SSR pages and SEO), TypeScript
  • Backend: Node.js with Express.js or NestJS, TypeScript
  • Database: PostgreSQL (structured analytics), Redis (caching/session)
  • Tracking & ETL: Server-side Node services, webhook processors
  • Infrastructure: AWS (EC2, Lambda, S3), Docker, GraphQL or REST APIs
  • Analytics & Metrics: Kafka or Kinesis pipelines, Redis aggregations
    • Use Case:

    An online retailer integrates Digital Sumo to track ad conversions from Facebook, Google, TikTok, and Quora. They utilize server-side tracking to improve attribution accuracy and understand channel effectiveness at each funnel stage.

    Challenges:
  • Cross-Channel Attribution: Consolidating data from disparate ad platforms
  • Tracking Accuracy: Overcoming ad-blockers and browser restrictions
  • Data Volume: Processing large-scale tracking and conversions in real time
  • UI Usability: Providing intuitive, flexible analytics dashboards
    • Solutions:
  • Unified Data Layer: Built ETL pipelines collecting conversion events from ads, sites, and webhooks
  • Server-Side Tracking: Bypassed browser-level blockers—improved attribution fidelity
  • Scalable Architecture: Used Kafka/Redis for real-time aggregation; AWS Lambda for event processing
  • Real-Time Dashboards: Developed React/Next UI with modular charts, custom filters, and exportable reports
    • Results:
    • ↑ 30–50% ROAS Lift: More accurate tracking led clients to reallocate budgets to higher-performing channels
    • ↑ 20+% Conversions: Optimized funnels and timely attribution drove conversion improvements
    • ↓ 15% Ad Spend Wastage: Insights removed underperforming ads quickly
    • Better Client Profit: Clients saw double-digit increase in net revenue attributed to analytics-driven marketing optimizations
